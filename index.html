Why you should start Climbing!

Climbing has grown in popularity over the past few years and what we, as certified instructors, have found is that providing a safe and beginner friendly enviroment for new or aspiring climbers is how we can help and make sure everyone is safe, secure and free to take climbing to new heights.

What we offer!

Kids & Teens

  • N.I.C.A.S

    A class where kids aged 6-18 can learn coordination, technique and other valuable skills in a social enviroment centered around climbing.

  • Bouldering Introduction

    Kids can learn how to boulder, safe practices and personalised coaching to improve.

  • Top Rope Introduction

    Learn the basics of rope climbing with a friend or an adult to reach new heights!

  • Climbing Camps

    During holidays we host climbing camps, where kids can come build friendships and learn climbing.

  • Sunday Sends

    Every sunday we host a class for kids to show us what they want to work on and improve and offer assistance and guidance.

Adults

  • Bouldering Introduction

    A beginner friendly class where you can learn what bouldering is, how to improve and approach the bouldering wall in a social enviroment.

  • Top Rope Introduction

    A hands on approach to rope climbing, where you learn how to belay and climb with a partner.

  • Lead Climbing Introduction

    A more advanced form of climbing that requires having done Top Rope Introduction and Boudler Introduction's. Lead climbing sets you up with the skills and knowledge to approach outside climbing.

  • Advanced Bouldering

    This is a personalised coaching lesson with one of our instructors and will showcase advanced techniques and training methods to get the most out of your sessions.

  • Safety and Climbing Etiquette

    This class is for Parents or Guardians who want to ensure safety on climbing trips, making people aware of problems that might arise and how to combat them.
